
All three judges scored the fight in favor of Godinez, who snapped a two-fight skid with the victory. A staple in the Top 15 for the last couple years, the action-oriented Godinez remains one of the most consistently exciting competitors in the 115-pound weight class.

Miranda was able to initiate a grappling sequence in the opening moments of the first round, working to attack an arm triangle choke off the bottom. But once Emmers cleared the submission and forced the Brazilian to stand, it was one-way traffic. A straight right hand dropped Miranda, a lead elbow put him on rubber legs soon after, and another series of blows along the fence finally brought the bout to a close.
