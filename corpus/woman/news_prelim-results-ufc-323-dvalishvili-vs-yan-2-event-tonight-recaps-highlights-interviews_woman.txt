
Maycee Barber ended a 21-month absence with an emotional victory over Karine Silva at UFC 323, earning a clean sweep of the scorecards to pick up where she left off at UFC 299.

Silva controlled much of the first round with her grappling, taking Barber to the canvas and hunting for chokes from back mount, only for the Contender Series grad to reverse into top position and finish the round landing hard shots. Barber maintained her momentum in the second and weathered an illegal upkick that landed flush to draw level before the two had very different positive moments in the third, with Barber dominating the striking exchanges and Silva threatening with leg locks when they found their way to the ground.

The judges were called upon to determine the victor and when the scorecards were collected and totaled, it was “The Future” than emerged triumphant. It’s been a long road back for Barber, but she’s now won seven straight, drawing level with Natalia Silva for the longest winning streak in the division and adding her name back into the title conversation.
