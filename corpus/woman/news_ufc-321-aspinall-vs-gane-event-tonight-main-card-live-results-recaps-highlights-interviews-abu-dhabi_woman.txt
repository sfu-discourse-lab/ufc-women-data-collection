
Unlike their first encounter five years ago, the standout grapplers did spend time on the canvas in their championship rematch, but once again, the deciding moments of this contest came on the feet, with Dern consistently landing the more individually impactful blows across multiple rounds. Jandiroba had success hitting takedowns, but failed to advance positions, do much damage or chase submissions, with Dern doing well to remain active off her back.

They went the distance, giving the pair eight full rounds together inside the Octagon overall, and when the judges’ scorecards were collected and tallied, it was Dern that came out ahead. A decade after registering the biggest victory of her Brazilian jiu jitsu career in Abu Dhabi, the 32-year-old replicated the act at UFC 321, claiming the vacant strawweight title with a hard-fought victory over an equally tough Jandiroba in Saturday’s penultimate bout.
