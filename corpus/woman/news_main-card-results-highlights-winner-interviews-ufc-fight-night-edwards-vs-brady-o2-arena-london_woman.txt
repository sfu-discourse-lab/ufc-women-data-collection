
Thainara danced to the Octagon and showed zero signs of nerves, taking McCann to the canvas, dominating on the mat, and eventually working to the back, where she hunted down the rear-naked choke. While McCann defended the initial attack, Thainara was undeterred, adjusting her positioning, sinking in the choke, and collecting the tap.

Following the contest, McCann announced her retirement, bidding farewell to the sport in front of the loving fans that have been in her corner throughout her career. She wrapped her career with a beautiful speech, and should continue to be an amazing ambassador for the sport in the UK and abroad.
