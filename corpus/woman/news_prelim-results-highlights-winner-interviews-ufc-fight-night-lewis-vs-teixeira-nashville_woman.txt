Kline was effective and efficient in the opening round, pressuring forward and peppering Martinez with strikes, keeping her Mexican foe off balance. Though Martinez had more success in the second, Kline remained in control, and in the third, the birthday girl connected with a left high kick that put Martinez on the deck, bringing the fight to a sudden, explosive stop.

After debuting up a division and showing moxie against Jasmine Jasudavicius, Kline is now 2-0 with a pair of finishes since returning to her natural surroundings at 115 pounds. Just 25 years old, the future is bright for “The Archangel.”
