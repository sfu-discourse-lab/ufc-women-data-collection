Over the years, UFC has been home to many walkouts that have left fans captivated, entertained and sometimes even intimidated.

But what makes a great walkout? The song choice, creativity and magnitude of the fight all play a role in this unranked, unordered list that features some of the most unforgettable walks to the Octagon.

The song, despite not being the usual upbeat tune, was featured in one of the most captivating walkouts in UFC history, drawing in spectators and making the event seem more than just a fight, but a spectacle.

The song opens with the lyrics, “If you are who you say you are, a superstar, then have no fear," a message that perfectly captured O'Malley’s mindset. He has always seen himself as a superstar, and that night, he proved it. With a second-round finish of the reigning champion, O'Malley claimed the title in emphatic fashion. Looking back, the walkout and the performance that followed have cemented this moment as one of the most iconic in UFC history.
