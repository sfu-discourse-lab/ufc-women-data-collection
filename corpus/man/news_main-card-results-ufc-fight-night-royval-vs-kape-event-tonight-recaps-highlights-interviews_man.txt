That’s now four wins this year and five straight victories overall for the Brazilian. “The Dalmatian” has gone 6-1 in his natural weight class after debuting on short notice up at lightweight, with his lone setback coming against streaking American finisher Steve Garcia. After a performance like this and the year he’s had, Costa could very well be joining his former opponent in the rankings in the new year.

Middleweight has been growing deeper over the last couple years, and Oleksiejczuk is definitely a long-term prospect to keep eyes on going forward.
