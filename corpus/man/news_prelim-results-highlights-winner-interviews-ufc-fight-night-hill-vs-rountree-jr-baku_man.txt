
Scores were 30-27 across the board for Ko, now 12-2. Elliott falls to 12-3.

Ko got off to a good start with his grappling attack, opening an early lead before Elliott responded in the second half of the first round.

The ground attack of Ko continued to pay dividends in the second, with the newcomer smoothly controlling Elliott throughout the frame.

Needing a finish in the third, Elliott wound up on the mat early, Ko continuing to impress as he soared to his first UFC win.

In the second, the fight appeared to come to an abbreviated end when Naurdiev landed an illegal knee on the grounded Park, cutting him over the eye and prompting referee Lukasz Bosacki to call a halt to the action. Park was cleared to continue, though, and Naurdiev was docked two points.

When the fight resumed, Park locked up with Naurdiev in search of a takedown, but Naurdiev resisted and proceeded to get his own takedown. Park was back to his feet with 90 seconds left, and he finally got the takedown, maintaining position on the mat until the horn.

In the third, Park dominated on the ground, nearly sinking in a rear-naked choke before the bout ended, securing his victory.

The crowd was engaged from the start as the two evenly matched 125-pounders battled it out. Ulanbekov was patient in his attack in the opening round, while Maksum was more aggressive with his striking while dismissing a series of slam attempts by his foe.

Ulanbekov, with Khabib Nurmagomedov and Islam Makhachev in his corner, was more aggressive in the second, but it was Maksum who ended the round with a takedown.

Scores were 29-28 across the board for Usman, now 12-4. Abdelwahab falls to 6-1, 1 NC.

Abdelwahab took an early lead on the strength of several kicks to the leg, and when he moved his attacks upstairs, nearly every shot seemed to draw a reaction from Usman. In the second, Usman reversed the trajectory of the bout with a pair of right hands that staggered Abdelwahab, and while he wasn’t able to capitalize, that sequence might have earned him the round. And with the fight likely up for grabs, Usman stung Abdelwahab late, securing the win on the scorecards.
