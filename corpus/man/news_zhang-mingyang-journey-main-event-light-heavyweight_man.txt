Zhang Mingyang first entered the UFC ecosystem back in June of 2022, when he competed in a non-tournament bout on the inaugural season of Road to UFC in Singapore, facing Tuko Tokkos. Heading into the fight, Tokkos was a massive betting favorite, but Zhang flipped the script quickly, ending the fight in the first four minutes.

The interesting angle to this fight is that Zhang and Walker have actually trained together. Prior to his bout against Smith, Zhang connected with Walker to help him prepare for “Lionheart”, given Walker’s experience also facing him.
