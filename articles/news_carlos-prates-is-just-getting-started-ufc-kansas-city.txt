In part since The Fighting Nerds have felt rather omnipresent for the last couple months, it’s surprising that Carlos Prates is approaching his first fight of 2025. The red-hot Brazil-based team rolled its massive momentum from 2024 into a showstopping start to 2025 between a trio of finishes from Jean Silva and Mauricio Ruffy, and in each appearance, the teammates who aren’t fighting—in this case Prates and Caio Borralho—are seen Octagonside celebrating each victory.
