Following a solid collection of action on the prelims, business at The O2 in London shifted to the main card, where the excitement picked up, and the night of mixed results for British fighters continued.

Ulberg entered on a seven-fight winning streak, facing off with a ranked opponent for just the second time, while the former champion Blachowicz was competing for the first time since July 2023, aiming to end a modest two-fight turn without a victory.
