
Maycee Barber was not medically cleared to compete after getting ready to make the walk, resulting in her bout with Erin Blanchfield being cancelled. Before the bout was called off, the action was ramping up, with the flyweight headlining act poised to be a thrilling crescendo.

The Brazilian, who had issues making weight, resulting in the fight being shifted to featherweight, was the better of the two on the feet in the first before using her significant top pressure to keep Chiasson stapled to the canvas in the second and third. Though Chiasson looked for opportunities to land off her back, as she did last time out against Mayra Bueno Silva, Vieira did well to stay tight and minimize the offense coming back her way until the very late stages of the fight.
