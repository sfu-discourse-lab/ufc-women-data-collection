
In the final two bouts of the prelims, Canadians Jasmine Jasudavicius and Mike Malott went back-to-back, with the former garnering a first-round submission win over Jessica Andrade, while the latter defended home turf with a second-round knockout of American Charles Radtke.

That’s now consecutive victories for the de facto captain of Team Canada, and his 11th finish in a dozen professional wins. After earning a measured decision win last year in Edmonton, this was a proper Malott performance in the fight capital of Canada.

The surging Canadian got Andrade’s attention early with a pair of long right hands that opened the door for a level change and high amplitude takedown close to the fence. From there, the Niagara Top Team representative worked to take the back, sink in her hooks, and flatten Andrade out, shifting to the choke after softening her up with punches.
