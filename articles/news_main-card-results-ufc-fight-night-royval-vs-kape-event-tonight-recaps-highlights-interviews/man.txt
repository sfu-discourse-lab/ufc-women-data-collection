
Costa has quietly had a tremendous year and closed it out in style here. That’s now four wins this year and five straight victories overall for the Brazilian. “The Dalmatian” has gone 6-1 in his natural weight class after debuting on short notice up at lightweight, with his lone setback coming against streaking American finisher Steve Garcia. After a performance like this and the year he’s had, Costa could very well be joining his former opponent in the rankings in the new year.

Manel Kape closed out the year in emphatic fashion, sparking Brandon Royval to add further intrigue to the top of the flyweight division.
