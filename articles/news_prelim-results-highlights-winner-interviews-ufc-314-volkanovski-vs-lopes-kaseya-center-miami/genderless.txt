Miami is a city known for its vibrancy and activity, and Saturday night at Kaseya Center, that’s what the men and women on the UFC 314 prelims brought with them to the Octagon.

It was an outstanding early slate that sets the stage for what should be an action-packed main card, and we have all the details from the preliminary card contests collected for your review below.

The main event and co-main event are scheduled for five rounds. All other bouts are scheduled for three rounds.

After alternating wins and losses through his first six UFC appearances at featherweight, “The Dream” has now garnered five straight wins since shifting to the 155-pound weight class. It’s taken some time, but the DWCS grad has developed into a menace, and is someone to keep close tabs on going forward in the second half of the year.

The judges were called upon for the first time in the evening, with two of the three seeing it in favor of the veteran flyweight. It’s a must-needed win for the 29-year-old Sumudaerji, who moved to 4-4 in the UFC and 17-7 overall with the victory.
