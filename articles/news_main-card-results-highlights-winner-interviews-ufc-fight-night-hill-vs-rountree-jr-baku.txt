The UFC Octagon landed in Baku, Azerbaijan, on Saturday, and fans at Baku Crystal Hall were treated to an action-packed card headlined by a clash of light heavyweight contenders Jamahal Hill and Khalil Rountree Jr. When the dust settled, it was Khalil Rountree Jr. securing a dominant win. Also emerging victorious on the main card were Rafael Fiziev, Curtis Blaydes, Myktybek Orolbai, Nazim Sadykhov and Muhammad Naimov.
