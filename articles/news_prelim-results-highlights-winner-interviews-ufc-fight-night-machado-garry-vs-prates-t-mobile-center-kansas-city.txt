Back in Kansas City for the first time in a couple years, the action on Saturday night started much more quickly than it did the last time the Octagon rolled into town.
UFC Fight Night: Machado Garry vs Prates Prelim Results
The bantamweights took the first 90 seconds to measure one another and find their ranges, but then as Saaiman threw a lunging lead right hook, Wellmaker countered inside with a thunderclap of a right that caught the South African clean and sent him dropping to the canvas.
Amorim wasted no time putting Viana on the canvas and kept her there for the duration of the first round, advancing to mount at one point while chasing submissions and landing damage. She took her down in the center of the cage immediately to start the second, working around to the back and locking in a rear-naked choke to secure the tap.
Cuamba was a little hesitant, but still effective in the first round, showing the speed he possesses in his hands while dealing with the aggressive offense coming back his way well. He got looser with his striking in the second, believing in his weapons more and more as each shot landed before timing up a jumping knee that caught Romero on the button and sent him tumbling to the canvas.
From the jump, “La Pantera” was on the offensive, pressuring forward and touching Chandler with long punches and hard body kicks, growing with confidence as the round ticked on. She continued to stalk forward and throw, and after backing Chandler into the fence, a left hand finally sent her crashing to the canvas, halting the action.
