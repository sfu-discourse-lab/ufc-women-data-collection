Things were going just fine for Trey Waters in his May 2024 bout against Billy Ray Goff until they weren’t, and when you tear the ACL and meniscus in your knee and you’ve still got 14 minutes of a fight to go, it has to be panic time. Well, maybe panic time for 99.9% of the population, but for Waters, he didn’t have time to panic, as Goff was trying to punch him in the face.
