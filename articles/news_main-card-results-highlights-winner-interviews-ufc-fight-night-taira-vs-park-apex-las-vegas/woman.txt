
The Brazilian, who has been a mainstay in the Top 15 for the last five years, ran level with the French Muay Thai stylist in the first before hitting a beautiful blast double to put Cornolle on the deck early in the second, keeping her there and piling up the punishment throughout the frame. She went straight back to the takedown in the third, once again keeping her French opponent stapled to the canvas for the majority of the round, chipping away with strikes while frequently looking to work towards an arm-triangle choke that never materialized.

All three judges saw the fight in favor of Rosa, who maintained her pattern of alternating results for the ninth consecutive fight. She remains just outside the elite set in the 135-pound weight class, but continues to thrive in the middle third of the rankings, while showing no signs of being ready to cede her position.
