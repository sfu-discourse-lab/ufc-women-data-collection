Rongzhu defeats Austin Hubbard by unanimous decision (30-27, 29-28, 29-28)
Lightweights Rongzhu and Austin Hubbard engaged in an entertaining back-and-forth midway through the preliminary card slate, with the young Chinese veteran claiming his second straight UFC victory in the end.
Rongzhu leaned on his superior speed and diversity of attacks, chopping at the lead leg of Hubbard in the opening round and snapping home clean punches throughout. The resilient American did well to up his output and wrestle more in the second, but Rongzhu came out pressing in the third, taking the fight to Hubbard and landing the more meaningful shots throughout.
All three judges scored the fight in favor of Rongzhu, who picked up his second win of the year. The 25-year-old continued to show improvements in his second stint with the promotion, and is an intriguing figure to track going forward in the 155-pound weight class.
