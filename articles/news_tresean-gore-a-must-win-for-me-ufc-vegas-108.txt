Tresean Gore is the first to admit that maybe he came off a bit too strong during his time on The Ultimate Fighter. He was oozing confidence on the show after earning two wins and going on to say that he would be a two-division champion in a year. Since then, it hasn’t been all sunshine and rainbows, as Gore now sits at 2-3 in the UFC. But for the 30-year-old, he believes he has grown leaps and bounds throughout his journey since the show.
