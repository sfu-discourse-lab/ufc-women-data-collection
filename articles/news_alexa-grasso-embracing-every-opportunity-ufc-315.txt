For the first time in two years, Alexa Grasso is preparing to face someone other than Valentina Shevchenko. The first time the two met was back in 2023, when Grasso pulled off one of the biggest upsets of the year, submitting the flyweight champion in the fourth round. The two went on to compete two more times after that, with Shevchenko defeating Grasso last September to reclaim her belt and concluding their championship trilogy.
