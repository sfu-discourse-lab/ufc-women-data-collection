The combatants that took to the Octagon to open Saturday’s fight card at Accor Arena in Paris gave the capacity crowd quite the show on the prelims, as every single bout ended inside the distance, setting the stage for what should be a captivating main card.


 
Sam Hughes kicked things off with a second-round submission win over Shauna Bannon before Rinat Fakhretdinov, Robert Bryczek, Sam Patterson, and Kaue Fernandes all chalked up technical knockout victories, with all but Bryczek handling their business in the opening round. That was followed by heavyweight newcomer Ante Delija collecting a first-round knockout win over Top 10 staple Marcin Tybura prior to hometown favorite Oumar Sy wrapping things up with a first-round stoppage win of his own.
