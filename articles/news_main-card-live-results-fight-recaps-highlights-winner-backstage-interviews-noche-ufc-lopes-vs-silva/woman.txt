
Lopes dominated the opening round, putting Silva on the canvas early and mauling him from mount for the majority of the frame. In the second, Silva came out aggressively, taking the fight to Lopes and landing big shots, looking for a spinning back elbow multiple times. Late in the round, with the two men trading big shots all around the Octagon, Silva pressed forward and Lopes spun, slicing the Fighting Nerds representative wide open before pouncing and pounding out the finish.
