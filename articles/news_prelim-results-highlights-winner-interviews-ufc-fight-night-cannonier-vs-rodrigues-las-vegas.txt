Following an entertaining preliminary card slate that featured a trio of finishes and some standout moments from emerging talents, Saturday’s main card continued to deliver excitement at the UFC APEX.


 
After a competitive middleweight clash between Andre Petroski and Rodolfo Vieira, Nazim Sadykhov and Edmen Shahbazyan turned in consecutive first-round finishes heading into the final two bouts of the day. In the co-main event, Youssef Zalal continued his winning ways, coming out of the gates sharp and holding on down the stretch against game veteran Calvin Kattar.
And then to close out the show, Jared Cannonier and Gregory Rodrigues teamed up for an absolutely slobberknocker in the middleweight division, with the Top 10 fixture collecting a thrilling fourth-round stoppage win.
It was another thoroughly entertaining evening in Las Vegas, and we have all the details collected for you below.

UFC Fight Night: Cannonier vs Rodrigues Prelim Results
Jacqueline Cavalcanti defeats Julia Avila by unanimous decision (30-27, 30-27, 30-27)
Ascending bantamweight Jacqueline Cavalcanti pushed her record to 4-0 inside the Octagon with a clean and clear decision win over Julia Avila in Saturday’s opener.
From the outset, the 27-year-old Portuguese fighter was the more effective of the two, utilizing her long, straight shots to chip away and batter Avila. She busted up the returning “Raging Panda” in the second and continued to dominate the exchanges in the third, never allowing the trailing Avila to build any momentum.

Valter Walker defeats Don’Tale Mayes by submission (heel hook) at 1:17 of Round 1

Walker wasted no time closing the distance, locking his hands, and depositing Mayes on the canvas. From there, “The Clean Monster” quickly shifted his focus to attacking the heel hook, rolling through and securing the tap in just over a minute.

Elijah Smith defeats Vince Morales by unanimous decision (29-28, 29-28, 29-28)
Newcomer Elijah Smith and veteran Vince Morales teamed up for an entertaining bantamweight clash in the middle of Saturday’s preliminary card action at the UFC APEX. 
Smith was the more effective of the two in the opening stanza, rallying from a hard low blow to out-strike Morales before the Las Vegas-based veteran drew level by controlling the second with his grappling, getting in deep on a couple different submission attempts. In the third, Smith blew up Morales’ nose with a clean uppercut, surviving a late bulldog choke attempt at the horn.

Rafael Estevam defeats Jesus Aguilar by unanimous decision (29-28, 29-28, 29-28)
Rafael Estevam returned to action for the first time in well over a year, maintaining his unbeaten record with a gritty decision win over fellow DWCS grad Jesus Aguilar.
The Nova Uniao product was strong in the early going, using his grappling to neutralize the offense of his Mexican counterpart, repeatedly putting Aguilar on the deck and keeping him there for extended periods over the first two rounds. In the third, Aguilar had much more success as Estevam tired, but the Brazilian was able to avoid getting finished and close out the fight in top position.
