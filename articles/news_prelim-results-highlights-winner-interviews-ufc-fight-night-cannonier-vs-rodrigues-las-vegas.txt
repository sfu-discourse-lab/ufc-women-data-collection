Following an entertaining preliminary card slate that featured a trio of finishes and some standout moments from emerging talents, Saturday’s main card continued to deliver excitement at the UFC APEX.
It was another thoroughly entertaining evening in Las Vegas, and we have all the details collected for you below.
Ascending bantamweight Jacqueline Cavalcanti pushed her record to 4-0 inside the Octagon with a clean and clear decision win over Julia Avila in Saturday’s opener.
This was another sharp effort for the streaking Cavalcanti, who has now won seven straight overall. She’s easily the most intriguing emerging name in the 135-pound weight class and should garner another step up in competition next time out after adding another one-sided win to her resume here.
Valter Walker is out here hitting heel hooks like they’re his finishing maneuver, securing his second straight win with the rarely seen submission in the early stages of his heavyweight clash with Don’Tale Mayes.
Newcomer Elijah Smith and veteran Vince Morales teamed up for an entertaining bantamweight clash in the middle of Saturday’s preliminary card action at the UFC APEX.
Gabriel Bonfim turned in his most impressive performance to date in the UFC, dominating and submitting Khaos Williams.
Rafael Estevam returned to action for the first time in well over a year, maintaining his unbeaten record with a gritty decision win over fellow DWCS grad Jesus Aguilar.
Veteran Angela Hill and up-and-comer Ketlen Souza locked up in a competitive battle in the strawweight division that went the full 15 minutes.
The MMA Lab has another promising prospect on their hands, as Jose Delgado rolled into his UFC debut and ran through Connor Matthews.
