UFC returns to UFC APEX with a thrilling middleweight matchup featuring No. 8 ranked contender Jared Cannonier facing off against Gregory Rodrigues. In addition, No. 10 ranked featherweight Calvin Kattar collides with the surging Youseff Zalal.


 
UFC FIGHT NIGHT: CANNONIER vs RODRIGUES takes place Saturday, February 15 in Las Vegas and will be available exclusively on ESPN+ (English and Spanish). The prelims will air at 4pm ET/1pm PT, followed by the main card at 7pm ET/4pm PT.
Main event scheduled for five rounds. All other bouts scheduled for three rounds.

UFC Fight Night: Cannonier vs Rodrigues Main Card Results
Andre Petroski defeats Rodolfo Vieira by unanimous decision (29-28, 29-28, 29-28)
Andre Petroski pushed his winning streak to three with a hard-fought decision win over Brazilian jiu jitsu ace Rodolfo Vieira.
The Philadelphia native did a tremendous job forcing Vieira to engage with him on the feet, stuffing numerous takedown attempts and getting the better of the striking exchanges in the first and third.

Nazim Sadykhov defeats Ismael Bonfim by TKO (doctor stoppage) at 5:00 of Round 1
A head kick in the late stages of the opening round brought the all-action bout between Nazim Sadykhov and Ismael Bonfim to an anticlimactic close between the first and second rounds.
Bonfim started well, showing the powerful striking that has defined the early stages of his UFC career, but Sadykhov slowly warmed his way into the contest, connecting with a question mark kick just as the Brazilian dipped low, catching him across the eyes.

Edmen Shahbazyan defeats Dylan Budka by TKO (strikes) at 1:35 of Round 1
All it took was one big shot for Edmen Shahbazyan to get himself back into the win column.
After just over a minute of action where both he and Dylan Budka pawed at one another, trying to find their range, Shahbazyan connected with a pull right that landed flush and shook the equilibrium of “The Mindless Hulk.” As Budka spilled to the floor, Shahbazyan pounced, hammering out the finish.

Co-Main Event: Youssef Zalal defeats Calvin Kattar by unanimous decision (29-28, 29-28, 29-28)
Youssef Zalal secured the biggest win of his professional career on Saturday, surviving a competitive battle with Top 10 stalwart Calvin Kattar to earn his fourth straight UFC victory.
Zalal used his speed, boxing, and movement to get out to an early lead against Kattar, picking him apart at range over the first 10 minutes. Spurred on by his corner ahead of the third, the veteran from Massachusetts came out more aggressive and had a great deal of success, forcing Zalal to constantly circle to open space and try to run out the clock.

Main Event: Jared Cannonier defeats Gregory Rodrigues by TKO (strikes) at 0:21 of Round 4
Jared Cannonier and Gregory Rodrigues engaged in a wildly entertaining back-and-forth to close out Saturday’s fight card, with Cannonier weathering the storm to secure a fourth-round stoppage win.
“Robocop” started like he was shot out of a cannon, taking the fight to the former title challenger and dropping him twice in the opening frame. Cannonier rebounded in the second, finding his range and connecting with big shots of his own.
