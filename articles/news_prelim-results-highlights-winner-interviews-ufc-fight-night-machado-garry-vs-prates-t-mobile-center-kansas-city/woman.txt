
Amorim wasted no time putting Viana on the canvas and kept her there for the duration of the first round, advancing to mount at one point while chasing submissions and landing damage. She took her down in the center of the cage immediately to start the second, working around to the back and locking in a rear-naked choke to secure the tap.

From the jump, “La Pantera” was on the offensive, pressuring forward and touching Chandler with long punches and hard body kicks, growing with confidence as the round ticked on. She continued to stalk forward and throw, and after backing Chandler into the fence, a left hand finally sent her crashing to the canvas, halting the action.
