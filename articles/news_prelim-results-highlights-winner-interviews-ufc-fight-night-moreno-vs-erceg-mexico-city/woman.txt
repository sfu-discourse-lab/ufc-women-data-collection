
Godinez leaned on her grappling in the first and second rounds to ground the dangerous Brazilian striker, slamming her to the canvas with force in both frames. Polastri landed good shots off her back in the first, and worked to walk down Godinez in the third, landing heaps of heavy shots that left the ranked veteran bloodied as they continued to battle through to the final horn.

All three judges scored the fight in favor of Godinez, who snapped a two-fight skid with the victory. A staple in the Top 15 for the last couple years, the action-oriented Godinez remains one of the most consistently exciting competitors in the 115-pound weight class.
