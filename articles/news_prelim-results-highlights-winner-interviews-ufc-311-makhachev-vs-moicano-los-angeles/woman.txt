
Perez did well to utilize her wrestling at opportune times, changing levels and putting Rosa on the deck in both the first and second, neutralizing the pressure the Brazilian was beginning to apply each time. Rosa had more success in the third, keeping things standing and throwing with more force and intent, but never getting close to creating a finishing opportunity.

When the scorecards were tallied, it was Perez that came out ahead, registering her fifth straight UFC victory. The charismatic Argentine is the owner of the longest active winning streak in the division, and should get another opportunity against a Top 10 opponent next time out after out-working the tenured Brazilian on Saturday night in Los Angeles.
